Chicken Momo

Chicken Momo is a popular dumpling dish famous in Tibet, Nepal, China and neighboring countries. Filled with a delicious mixture of minced chicken, onions, ginger, and a blend of aromatic spices.

These momos are typically encased in a thin dough wrapper and are either steamed, fried, or a combination of both, resulting in a delightful texture and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Minced Chicken
  • Minced Onions
  • Ginger
  • Oil
  • Monosodium Glutamate
  • Thin Dumpling Wrappers
  • Dalle Paste
  • Garlic
  • Momo Masala

Steps:

  1. Prepare the filling by combining minced chicken, minced onions, ginger, MSG and momo masala in a bowl.
  2. Place a small portion of the filling onto a thin dumpling wrapper.
  3. Fold and seal the dumpling to create a momo shape.
  4. Repeat the process for the remaining wrappers and filling.
  5. For steamed momos, steam them until cooked through. For fried momos, heat oil in a pan and fry until golden brown.
  6. Prepare the dipping sauce by mixing Dalle paste, garlic, soy sauce, and vinegar.
  7. Serve the momos hot with the dipping sauce.
