Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Chicken Fried Rice is a delicious and flavorful dish made with cooked rice, tender chicken pieces, vegetables, and a blend of savory sauces. This classic fried rice recipe is quick to prepare and perfect for a satisfying meal.

The combination of seasoned chicken, fresh vegetables, and aromatic fried rice creates a delightful dish that's loved by many.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • Chicken breast or thigh, diced
  • Vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, corn)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, grated
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan and saut√© minced garlic and grated ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add diced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. Push the chicken to the side of the pan and pour beaten eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until cooked.
  4. Add vegetables to the pan and stir-fry until they are tender.
  5. Add cold cooked rice to the pan and mix well with the other ingredients.
  6. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Continue to stir-fry until everything is well combined and heated through.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
